Pouce

Packaging - Illustration - Edition

Pouce is a box that helps you start your day well ! You’re going to work without having taken the time to have breakfast ? Don’t panic, Pouce is there. Pastries, a drink, a newspaper with only good news and little surprises just for you! Pouce is also a completely reusable box with compartments for growing plants and others for storing your pens, or just somethingto make a pretty decoration at your office !

Workshop with Anyways Creative Studio, on the theme of the International day of Happiness. In collaboration with Morgane Massiani and Célia Martinez.
