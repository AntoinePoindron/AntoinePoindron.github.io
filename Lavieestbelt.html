La vie est Belt

Visual identity - Photography - Digital communication

Here is our reinterpretation of the visual identity of this eco - responsible brand that makes belts, pants, and even bow ties from recycled tyres ! So let’s do what they do, let’s go in the right direction !

Workshop with Benjamin Caron, and the french brand La vie est belt. In collaboration with Inès Davodeau, Tim Vanhill and Morgane Massiani.
